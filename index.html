Annotation

Here is presented the printed version of the book Hobbit, the first novel of series of books about Middle-Earth world. It was first published in 1937 in Great Britain and is still popular among people of all ages.

Description

Once there lived an ordinary hobbit in Shir. His life was rather good but a little bit boring. His name was Bilbo Baggins. One day his old friend, Gandalf, and a dozen and a half dwarves came to him and invited him to go with them to return back the Old dwarves' kingdom which was situated in The Lonely mountain. It was one of the richest countries with much gold. But one day a big dragon, Smaug, had destroyed this country. The mountain had been locked and Smaug guarded the entrance. That was a problem. The dwarves and Wizard Gandalf offered the hobbit to be a burglar of the entrance to the mountain in their company. But what could a little hobbit do? Would he be able to cope with the dangers, get along with harsh dwarves and handle with his role in this operation..?

Oleg Komrakov

19.03.2023

“Hobbit” by J. R. R. Tolkien – adventurous story, that goes from child’s fairy tale with dwarfs, wizard and magic animals to epic Bottle of the Five Armies. It contains well established proportion of humor and tragedy with amazing (for its genre and auditory) amount of references to various literature sources, especially Middle Age Nordic and German. Story of mysterious and sneaky dragon, lying on his cursed gold, reminds of legends about Sigurd. Wizard Gandalf clearly reminds of Odin and his role in Nordic mythology. But the main protagonist – Bilbo Baggins is an ordinary, common man, living in his small and comfortable hideout, who is dragged in a series of epic deeds and become a great adventurer and explorer. It’s like if Mr. Pickwick turns into Allan Quatermain. So “Hobbit” is a great example of masterpiece writing, pleasing both interest for fascinating plot and thirst for intellectual quiz.
